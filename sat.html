Topic: Advanced Math

Which of the following equations represents a line that is parallel to the line with equation \( y = -3x + 4 \)?
A 6x + 2y = 15
B 3x - y = 7
C 2x - 3y = 6
D x + 3y = 1
AI Explanation

Correct! Parallel lines have the same slope. The given line has a slope of -3. Let's rewrite option A in slope-intercept form: 2y = -6x + 15 ➔ y = -3x + 7.5. Since the slope is -3, it is parallel.
